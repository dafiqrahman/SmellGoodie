About


Smell Goodie is a small natural bath and body products company located in eastern North Carolina. Established to create quality natural soaps at affordable prices, all of our products are made by hand with organic materials. Our philosophy, is that to truly feel good, you must feel good. As skin is your largest bodily organ, like any other, it must be well nourished to remain healthy. Like the nutrients you put into your body, so must you be mindful about what products you place onto your body. With our products, you can rest assured that you are using quality, natural, hand-made products, and all at an affordable price!
